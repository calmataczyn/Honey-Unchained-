How the model works

Honey Unchained is a full‑stack beekeeping program: we deploy modern hives, train farmers, guarantee offtake, and elevate product with premium Afro‑urban branding. Our fair‑trade, traceable supply chain moves raw nectar to global shelves — ethically.

1) Modern hives

Flow Hives increase yield, reduce bee disturbance, and improve purity — enabling more frequent, cleaner harvests.

2) Training & co‑ops

Hands‑on apiculture training and coop aggregation improve consistency, bargaining power, and logistics.

3) Traceable supply

Batch‑level traceability from hive → jar, aligned with fair‑trade standards and export compliance.

4) Premium markets

D2C in the U.S./India and B2B bulk exports. Design‑forward packaging built for conscious consumers.
